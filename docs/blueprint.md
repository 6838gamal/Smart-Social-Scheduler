# **App Name**: SoloStream

## Core Features:

- Dashboard Overview: Displays a summary of performance, upcoming posts, and recent interactions.
- AI Content Assistant: Generates post captions and suggests optimal posting times using AI to enhance reach and engagement. It uses the 'tone' as a tool when deciding which approach to use when generating captions
- Unified Inbox: Aggregates messages, comments, and replies from multiple social platforms into a single feed with AI-driven reply suggestions.
- Content Library: Stores and organizes past content, visuals, and templates for easy reuse, with AI-powered search capabilities.
- Analytics & Insights: Visualizes performance trends with charts and provides AI-driven recommendations for smarter posting decisions.
- Platform Integrations: Connects and manages multiple social media platforms, such as Telegram and X, with connection status monitoring.
- Smart Scheduling: Schedule your posts by letting AI pick the time automatically or by selecting a custom time manually.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Soft lavender (#E6E6FA) to evoke a sense of calm and creativity.
- Background color: Light gray (#F5F5F5) to maintain a clean, minimalist aesthetic.
- Accent color: Teal (#008080) to highlight key actions and AI suggestions.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a grotesque-style sans-serif, for a modern and neutral look. 
- Use clean, minimalist icons that align with a calm and intelligent user experience.
- Maintain a well-spaced, elegant layout that maximizes clarity and minimizes visual clutter.
- Subtle animations to enhance user experience, such as a light lavender glow on button hovers.
